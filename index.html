About Us

Space ODT specialises in the following applications:

Heavens from Earth

  • Adaptive-optics for ground-based VIS/NIR telescopes
  • Monte Carlo, physical-optics and analytic simulations
  • Pupil and focal-plane wave-front sensing
  • Real-time processing

Earth from Heavens

  • Phase-retrieval techniques for space-borne applications
  • Optical deblurring via data-driven processing
